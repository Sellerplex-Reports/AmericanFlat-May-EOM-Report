Strategic Summary

How DSP Contributed to Business Outcomes in May 2026

■ New Customer Acquisition: DSP delivered 84.1% NTB purchase rate — vs. ~78.6% for Sponsored Ads only — reaching net-new audiences outside the search funnel.


■ Cross-Channel Lift: SA→DSP and DSP→SA journeys achieved 9.65–11.54× ROAS, significantly outperforming single-channel paths.


■ Audience Amplification: Ad-exposed users generated 7.1× more revenue. Multi-frequency exposure drove 82.3% of total DSP-attributed sales.


■ Ramp Context: With only 17 active days, all metrics reflect an early learning phase. As audiences mature and retargeting pools deepen, ROAS is expected to improve through Q3.
